Peak Travel Seasons and Hotspots

Lets explore through the years and discover the peak and off peak seasons across the states and the effect of the Covid-19 pandemic.

In 2020, the world was in shambles, when Covid-19 was introduce to the world, with global and nationwide lockdowns.

Travel restrictions has limited the number of foreign arrivals entering the country by approximately 99.96% as pointed out in April of 2020. This continue in 2021 with minimal travelling throughout the year.

However, Malaysia tourism gradually increase in millions of tourist arrival as the world recover from Covid-19 outbreak.

Overall, Johor and Selangor continue to be the heart of tourism hotspots with Terengganu being the least with less than 20 tourist entry throughout the 4 years.
